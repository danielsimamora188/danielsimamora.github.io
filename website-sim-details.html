Information Details

  • Category: Website SIM SV IPB
  • Periode: Feb 2021 - April 2021

Website that presents material of management information system and business support information systems. This website also provides business support simulation features, namely:

  • Simulation of marketing information system.
  • Simulation of manufacturing information system.
  • Simulation of human resource information system.
  • Simulation of financial information system.
  • Simulation of accounting information system.
The website consists of 2 actors, including the user and the admin. The user is a vocational student of IPB, and the admin is a lecturer. This website was created to support learning for IPB vocational students during the pandemic.
